  • {% trans %}Managing your account and access to the platform{% endtrans %}

{% trans %}The processing of your data is based on your explicit consent, given at the time of account creation. You may withdraw this consent at any time by deleting your account.{% endtrans %}

{% trans %}To exercise these rights, contact us at{% endtrans %} privacidade@eureka.co {% trans %}or delete your account directly from your profile page.{% endtrans %}
